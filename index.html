Frequently asked questions

How is TaskTimeline Pro different from Asana or Monday.com?

TaskTimeline Pro charges a flat monthly rate ($19–$199) regardless of team size. Asana charges $10.99/user/month and Monday.com starts at $9/user. For a 10-person team, TaskTimeline Team ($49/mo) saves you $60+/month vs Asana.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. The free plan includes 1 project, 10 tasks, shareable links, and JSON export — forever, no credit card required. Upgrade only when you need more.

How long is the free trial for paid plans?

All paid plans (Pro, Team, Business) include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start your trial.

Can I export my project data?

Yes. Free users export to JSON. Pro and above export to CSV and PDF. All plans support shareable public links. Your data is always yours — no lock-in.

Does it work for remote or distributed teams?

Absolutely. TaskTimeline Pro is built for remote teams — real-time collaboration, share-by-link timelines, and no software to install. Works in any browser on any device.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your billing dashboard with one click. You keep full access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, ever.
